About

I grew up on a farm in South East Australia and did all the things any farmer would do, from riding horses, raising and milking cattle to cutting and baling hay.

After school I completed an apprenticeship as a Boilermaker and SSF, worked in different parts of Australia doing that, then decided to return to school and got an Advanced Certificate in Horticulture through the University of Melbourne (Aust).I worked in different feilds of Horticulture, growing Beans in NSW, Hydroponic Tomatoes and Canteloupes in Victoria and was an Assistant Store Manager for a major Retail chain (Bunnings Aust.) overseeing the Garden Department as one of my duties. After I met my wife (she is a FLorist) I made the move to the USA. Since then I have been an ASM for the Home Depot, a Supervisor for Quality Control (This was cool, I was a weld inspector for a company that supplied panels for some of the new military ships coming out of Mobile, AL built by AUSTAL) and finally made my way to working for the USPS

My hobbies iclude working on our classic '86 Porsche 928, building models, but my main passion right now is learning how to write code, especially web design and Front End work. I am currently enrolled in Codecademy studying the career path for Front End Engineer.
