Workshop Manual I made using Swift and Xcode

I wrote this small App to help me with my other passion, Restoring our Classic Porsche. I had the PDF of the manual but it was difficult to find the sections I wanted quickly, so I created the App to allow me to jump to a particular section and find the info quicker

Workshop Manual is as the name suggests, a workshop manual for the Porsche 928. I allows me to jump directly to the section I want then to scroll though the PDF without having to scroll endlessly to find what I want.
