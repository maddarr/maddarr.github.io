The Purposes/ Aims of the Association are:

To promote in the general community a knowledge of and enthusiasm for, the historical, cultural and environmental heritage of Erica; Including, but not limited to the towns and settlements of her districts.

Namely, Moondarra, Rawson, Parkers Corner, Coopers Creek, Jubilee, Jacobs Creek, Caringal, Gould and Tyers Junction. We do this by way of :

  • The collection, and preservation of materials of historic interest related to the area, including oral histories.

  • The provision of a library and archives collection.

  • The dissemination of knowledge of Erica and her districts history through a newsletter, publications, excursions and all other possible means.

  • The encouragement of the renovation, preservation and maintenance of historical sites and buildings.
